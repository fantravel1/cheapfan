‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much do WNBA tickets cost compared to NBA tickets?

WNBA tickets typically range from $25-60 for good seats, compared to $90-300+ for NBA tickets at the same arenas. This represents a 60-80% savings for watching elite basketball in identical venues. The Indiana Fever (Caitlin Clark's team) is the exception, with tickets now averaging $85.

Are women's sports ticket prices going up?

Yes, significantly. The 2024 WNBA season saw 150%+ price increases for popular teams like Indiana Fever and Chicago Sky due to rookie stars like Caitlin Clark and Angel Reese. NWSL and PWHL are also seeing year-over-year increases of 20-30%. Early adoption is recommended before prices normalize higher.

What is the best value women's sports league to attend?

NWSL (women's soccer) currently offers the best value with tickets averaging $20-45 for excellent sightlines. The league features World Cup and Olympic stars at a fraction of men's MLS prices. Soccer-specific stadiums mean every seat has great views.

Which WNBA teams have the cheapest tickets?

Teams like Atlanta Dream, Dallas Wings, and Los Angeles Sparks typically offer tickets starting at $15-25. However, avoid games against Indiana Fever or any Caitlin Clark matchup, as those command premium prices across all venues‚Äîoften 40-80% higher than other games.

Is the quality of play worth it in women's sports?

Absolutely. Women's sports feature elite athletes, Olympic gold medalists, and world champions. The WNBA showcases some of the purest basketball fundamentals in the sport. NWSL has World Cup winners. PWHL has Olympic hockey stars. The value comes from lower historical demand and media coverage, not lower quality.

When is the best time to buy women's sports tickets?

For most teams, 2-4 hours before game time offers the best last-minute deals. Exception: high-demand teams (Fever, Aces, Liberty) should be purchased 1-2 weeks in advance for better selection. Weeknight games are 25-40% cheaper than weekends. Avoid season openers and rivalry matchups.
