Crypto.com Arena Budget Survival Guide

Parking

Don't. Take Metro to Pico Station (walk 5 min). Or park at Union Station ($8) and take Metro. Arena parking is $30-50.

Food

Eat before. A burger inside is $18. Grand Central Market is 10 min away - get tacos for $4. Bring an empty water bottle (fill inside).

Best Cheap Seats

Section 302-305 and 332-335 have the best sightlines for upper deck. Avoid corners (301, 318, 319, 334) - obstructed views.

Arrive Early

Doors open 90 min before tip. Upper deck has a great view of warmups. Watch LeBron's shooting routine for free from your cheap seat.

Lakers Budget FAQ

What's the absolute cheapest I can see the Lakers?

Our verified lowest was $67 for Lakers vs. Jazz on a Tuesday in November 2024. Realistically, budget $70-85 for the cheapest home games, or $55-65 if you're willing to travel to Sacramento, Portland, or San Antonio to see them away.

Is it worth it to sit in the 300 level?

Absolutely. Crypto.com Arena has excellent sightlines even in the upper deck. Sections 302-305 (center court) give you a perfect tactical view of plays developing. You'll see basketball better than most lower-level corner seats.

When do Lakers tickets drop the most?

Two windows: (1) 48-72 hours before game when season ticket holders realize they can't go, and (2) 90-120 minutes before tip when sellers panic. The second window is more reliable but riskier if the game sells out.

Lakers vs. Clippers: which is cheaper?

Clippers at Intuit Dome average 35% cheaper than Lakers. If you just want to see NBA basketball in LA, Clippers are the budget play. But for the Lakers-specific experience (history, energy, LeBron), there's no substitute.
