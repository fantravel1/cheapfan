Yankee Stadium Budget Survival

Getting There

Take the 4, B, or D train to 161st Street-Yankee Stadium. $2.90 each way. Do not drive. Parking is $45-65 and traffic is brutal.

Eating Before

Walk to 161st Street for Dominican food and cheaper eats. Stadium food is $8-15 per item. Eat before and save $25-40.

Best Cheap Seats

Bleachers (203 for Creatures, 201/237 for quieter). Grandstand 420-428 behind home. Avoid the corner 400s - obstructed views.

Beer Prices

Cheapest option: 16oz domestic in the bleachers ($12). Everywhere else is $14-18. Pre-game at Stan's Sports Bar across the street.

Yankees Budget FAQ

What's the cheapest way to see the Yankees?

Tuesday night bleacher tickets vs. rebuilding teams (A's, Royals, Marlins, Tigers). We've seen $18-22 regularly. Buy on Gametime 2-3 hours before first pitch for best prices.

Is the bleacher experience worth it?

Absolutely. The Bleacher Creatures in Section 203 are legendary. Roll call, chanting, maximum energy. Many die-hard fans prefer bleachers to expensive seats. It's the most authentic Yankees fan experience for the lowest price.

Should I ever go to a Red Sox game?

If budget is your priority, no. Red Sox games are 5-6x the price of weak opponent games. The atmosphere is electric, but you can have amazing Yankees experiences for $25 that would cost $175 vs. Boston. Save the rivalry for a special occasion.
