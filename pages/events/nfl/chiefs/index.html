Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest way to see the Chiefs play?

Training camp at Missouri Western State is free and offers close player access. For regular season, target NFC opponents (Panthers, Falcons, Cardinals on rotation) in December - we've verified tickets as low as $89. Preseason games average $45 with the same Arrowhead atmosphere.

How far in advance should I buy Chiefs tickets?

For non-premium opponents (NFC rotation teams), wait until 3-5 days before game day when season ticket holders panic-sell. For Bills, Cowboys, or divisional games, buy 2-3 weeks out - these rarely drop. Thursday night games see steeper last-minute drops (23% average).

Is the Arrowhead tailgate worth arriving early for?

Absolutely. Lots open 4 hours before kickoff and the tailgate scene is considered the best in the NFL. The BBQ culture is genuine - walk the lots and you'll likely get offered food. Parking is $60 at the stadium but $30 in residential areas a mile out.

Are upper deck seats at Arrowhead worth it?

Yes - Arrowhead's bowl design means upper deck has excellent sightlines and the noise is actually louder up there (sound rises). Sections 340-345 are known as the loudest in the stadium. You'll see formations better than lower rows and save $100+.
