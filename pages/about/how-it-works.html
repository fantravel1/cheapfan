Our Evidence System

Not all hacks are created equal. We built a tier system so you know exactly how reliable each strategy is before you bet your weekend on it.

Tier A

Verified & Reliable

Multiple confirmed sources, officially documented, or verified by CheapFan team members personally. These work.

Example: "Student rush tickets at MSG are released 2 hours before tip-off at the Box Office"
95%+ Success Rate
Tier B

Well-Documented

Consistent fan reports, documented patterns, or verified by multiple independent sources over time.

Example: "Prices drop 40% on average in the final hour before NFL kickoff on secondary markets"
80%+ Success Rate
Tier C

Community Reported

Multiple fan reports that haven't been officially verified. Generally reliable but circumstances may vary.

Example: "The parking lot on 3rd Street usually has $10 spots if you arrive 90 minutes early"
65%+ Success Rate
Tier D

Experimental

Single reports or new strategies that need more testing. Use with backup plans ready.

Example: "Someone reported the team store has unadvertised clearance every Tuesday"
Needs More Data
