We take quality seriously. Here's what makes a hack worthy of CheapFan and what evidence we look for.

What Makes a Good Hack

    Actionable

    Clear steps that any fan can follow, not vague suggestions.

    100% above-board. No scams, no deception, no rule-breaking.

  • ‚úì
    Repeatable

    Works consistently, not just a one-time lucky break.

  • ‚úì
    Significant Savings

    Saves at least 15-20% compared to the standard approach.

    Verified to work within the last 6 months.

  • Tier 1
    Verified with Receipts

  • Tier 2

    Single Source

    One credible report with detailed methodology.

  • Tier 4
    Unverified

    Promising but awaiting confirmation. Clearly labeled.
