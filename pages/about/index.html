The Story Behind CheapFan

It started with a $400 ticket to a playoff game. A ticket that cost more than a month of groceries. A ticket that forced someone to choose between being there for their team and being responsible with their finances.

That's when we asked: Why does being a fan have to be so expensive?

We started researching. Talking to fans. Documenting strategies. And we discovered something incredible: there's a whole underground economy of fan knowledge‚Äîhacks, tricks, timing strategies, and creative approaches that let dedicated fans attend events for a fraction of the "normal" cost.

The problem? This knowledge was scattered. Hidden in Reddit threads. Passed down in group chats. Known only to the most dedicated superfans.

CheapFan is our answer. A centralized, verified, constantly-updated knowledge base of every legitimate way to attend live events on a budget.
