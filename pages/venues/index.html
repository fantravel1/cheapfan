Quick Venue Tips

Universal hacks that work at almost every stadium and arena.

Eat Before You Enter

Stadium food averages $15-25 per item. Eat a full meal before the game and you'll save $30-50 per person. Most venues allow one sealed water bottle.

Park Further, Save More

Official stadium parking runs $30-60. Park 10-15 minutes away and pay $10-15 instead. Bonus: faster exit after the game.

Download the App First

Every venue app has mobile-only deals, upgrade offers, and seat relocation features. Download before you arrive - stadium WiFi is always terrible.

Clear Bag Policy = Faster Entry

Most venues require clear bags. Bring one and skip the bag check line. Small clutches (4.5" x 6.5") usually exempt.

Last Inning / 4th Quarter Deals

Many concession stands discount food late in games. Hot dogs, popcorn, and soft pretzels often 50% off in the final period.
