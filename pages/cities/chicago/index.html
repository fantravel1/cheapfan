Getting Around Chicago

The CTA is your best friend. $2.50 per ride to anywhere.

  • Soldier Field: Green/Orange Line to Roosevelt, walk 15 min
  • United Center: Blue Line to Illinois Medical District
  • Wrigley Field: Red Line to Addison (literally at the stadium)
  • Guaranteed Rate: Red Line to 35th-Sox

Never pay for parking. Stadium parking is $30-60. CTA from anywhere is $2.50. Do the math.
