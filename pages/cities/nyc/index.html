üèüÔ∏è The MSG Problem (And Solutions)

Madison Square Garden is iconic. It's also the most expensive arena in America. Knicks and Rangers both play here. Both are brutal on budgets.

The Reality

  • Knicks median: $145 (highest NBA)
  • Rangers median: $125 (top-5 NHL)
  • Concerts: Even worse
  • "MSG experience" premium is real

The Hacks

  • Tuesday Knicks: 25-30% cheaper than weekends
  • Bad opponent: Pistons, Wizards = $75-90 possible
  • Rangers weeknight: Non-rival can hit $65-80
  • WNBA Liberty: MSG experience, $30-50 tickets
  • College basketball: Big East tourney, $40-60

Or... just go to Barclays Center. Same sports, 50-70% cheaper, easier to get to from Manhattan than you think.
