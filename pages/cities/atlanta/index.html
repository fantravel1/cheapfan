Mercedes-Benz Stadium changed American sports forever with fan-first food pricing. Here's why it matters.

Why It Works

MBS dropped prices 50%+ and saw food revenue INCREASE by 16%. Fans buy more when prices aren't insulting. Other stadiums are slowly following. But Atlanta was first, and the entire in-stadium experience benefits. A family of 4 can eat for $25 instead of $80.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Mercedes-Benz Stadium food prices really that cheap?

Yes. $2 hot dogs, $2 refillable sodas, $3 pizza slices, $5 domestic beers. This applies to both Falcons and Atlanta United games. MBS pioneered "fan-first pricing" and it works - a family of 4 can eat for $20-25 vs $80+ at most NFL stadiums. Revenue actually increased because fans buy more at fair prices.

What's the cheapest pro sport in Atlanta?

Atlanta Hawks at State Farm Arena. Upper level weeknight tickets start at $15, making it one of the cheapest NBA experiences in America. The recently renovated arena includes unique features like a nightclub and barbershop. Trae Young is a must-see talent at bargain prices.

Is Atlanta United worth it if I'm not a soccer fan?

Absolutely. Atlanta United games at Mercedes-Benz Stadium are one of America's best live sports experiences regardless of sport. 70,000+ fans, European-style supporter culture, the Viking Clap, and MBS's $2 hot dogs. Tickets start at $25. Many attendees became soccer fans after their first United game. The atmosphere converts skeptics.
