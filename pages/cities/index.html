How to Pick Your Destination

Multi-Team Cities

New York, LA, Chicago have multiple teams. Stack games on one trip - see 3-4 teams in one weekend to maximize travel value.

Budget-Friendly Markets

Tampa, Oakland, Cincinnati have lower ticket costs. Same sport, fraction of the price of premium markets.

Walkable Cities

Boston, Chicago, Philadelphia have downtown arenas. No car needed = $50-100 saved on parking and transport.

Away Game Destinations

See your team on the road. Lakers in Portland, Yankees in Baltimore - often 50% cheaper than home.
